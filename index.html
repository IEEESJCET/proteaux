What is Proteaux

Proteaux is a boulevard where entrepreneurs come together and share their journey, innovators present their awe-inspiring ideas and students to develop a well-built network. Here you will get an opportunity to learn, collaborate and explore the diverse entrepreneurial cultures.

Our Events

Powwow -Panel Discussion

Panel discussion on the topic of innovation and entrepreneurship skills aimed at introducing students to this era of innovation and technologies and to make them understand the wide range of possibilities hidden in it. Our mesmerising panel of speakers will exchange their views and ideas on the topic.

Prayaan-Experience Sharing

Inspiration awakens us to new possibilities by allowing us to transcend our ordinary experiences and limitations. So as the second part, we present to you a story telling session by successful entrepreneurs about their journey and an insight into the two worlds they have experienced.

Panacea - Design Challenge

Adding to the cornucopia of the events is an idea pitching contest for the students to portray their own innovative ideas. A weeklong contest consisting of 2 rounds will be a team battle.
